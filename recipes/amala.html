Amala with Ewedu Soup, Gbegiri and Orishirishi


Ingredient

  • Yam flour
  • Water
  • Ewedu leaves
  • Beans
  • Palm Oil

Steps

  1. Mix flour with cold water in a bowl
  2. Bring water to a boil in a pot
  3. Add the flour mixture to the boiling water
  4. Stir continuously to avoid lumps
  5. Cook until the mixture has a uniform consistency
  6. Add some of the reserved boiled water
  7. Turn the mixture again on fire to combine
  8. Press the amala to the sides of the pot to make it fluffy
